# Concept - Medieval Forest Runner

## Overview
**Medieval Forest Runner** is an endless runner game with a 2D Classic aesthetic, where the player assumes the role of an adventurer in a dangerous medieval forest.

## Theme and Aesthetics
- **Environment**: Medieval Forest with a Parallax system (Multiple depth layers).
- **Style**: 2D Classic (Side View).
- **Colors**: Tones of green, brown, and blue, with high contrast to highlight obstacles and enemies.

## Character: The Adventurer
The player controls an adventurer equipped with a sword.

### Movement Mechanics
1. **Run**: The character's default state.
2. **Jump**: To avoid ground obstacles and hit aerial enemies.
3. **Crouch/Slide**: To avoid high-altitude obstacles or low-flying enemies.

### Combat Mechanics
1. **Ground Attack**: Sword swing while running.
2. **Air Attack**: Sword swing during a jump.
3. **Crouch Attack**: Sword swing while crouching.

## Enemies (Animals)
1. **Fly**: Aerial enemy. Can be dodged by crouching or defeated with an air attack.
2. **Slug**: Ground enemy. Can be jumped over or defeated with a ground/crouch attack.

## Scoring System
- **Distance**: Points earned as the player advances.
- **Kills**: Bonus points for defeating enemies with the sword.

## Progression
- Gradual increase in game speed (Parallax and obstacle movement).
- Emergence of more complex enemy patterns.
